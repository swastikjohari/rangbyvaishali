About the Artist

Vaishali is a passionate artist whose bold use of colour and expressive brushwork brings every canvas to life.

From serene seascapes to vibrant spiritual art, her work spans portraits, landscapes, and Indian mythology — each piece telling its own story through rich acrylics and fearless colour palettes.

"Rang" means colour in Hindi — and this gallery is her way of sharing those colours with the world. Every painting is original, handmade, and one of a kind.
