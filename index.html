Shipping & Delivery

All paintings are carefully packed with bubble wrap and cardboard reinforcement. Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days within India. Tracking details are shared via WhatsApp once the order is dispatched.

Refund Policy

Since every painting is a one-of-a-kind original artwork, we do not accept returns or exchanges. However, if your painting arrives damaged during transit, please contact us within 48 hours with photos and we will resolve it promptly.

About the Artist

Vaishali is a passionate artist whose bold use of colour and expressive brushwork brings every canvas to life. From serene seascapes to vibrant spiritual art, each piece tells its own story through rich acrylics and fearless colour palettes.
